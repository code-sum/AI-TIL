- Language Field
  - World is 3D-property space
  - Object's property is actually in 3D-space: geometry, texture(color, reflectivity), physics.
  - NeRF&3DGS: only restores the texture (color & density) of objects, without recovering any semantic properties.
- LERF: Language Embedded Radiance Fields
  - Motivation
    - NeRF reconstructs geometry & texture but lacks semantic understanding.
  - Method
    - Embed CLIP features into NeRF as a multi-scale 3D language field.
