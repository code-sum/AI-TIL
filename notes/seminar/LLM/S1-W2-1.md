### [ACL 2025] LLMs know their vulnerabilities: Uncover Safety Gaps through Natural Distribution Shifts [🗂️](https://aclanthology.org/2025.acl-long.1207)

- 3줄 요약

  - 본 논문은 겉보기에는 무해해 보이지만 실제로는 유해한 맥락과 연결된 natural distribution shifts 를 이용해 LLM의 안전 장치를 우회할 수 있는 새로운 취약점을 규명함.
  - 이를 입증하기 위해 행위자-연결망 이론에 기반한 ActorBreaker 라는 멀티턴 공격 기법을 제안하여, 기존 방식보다 더 효과적이고 효율적으로 모델이 유해한 정보를 생성하도록 유도.
  - 이러한 위협에 대응하고자 ActorBreaker로 구축한 데이터셋을 통해 모델을 미세 조정(Fine-tuning)한 결과, 모델의 일반적인 성능(Utility)은 다소 감소했으나 안전성(Robustness)은 크게 향상됨.

- Background

  - LLMs are pre-trained on vast internet data, which unfortunately includes harmful and toxic content.
  - safe 하게 align 된 LLM 이라면 유해한 분야 질문에 대해 답변을 생성하면 안됨.
    - 이게 안되면 jailbroken 되었다고 말함.

- Introduction

  - Most vulnerability research focuses on "malicious distribution shifts": tricking LLMs by intentionally modifying harmful prompts.
  - This paper investigates a more subtle and realistic threat "Natual Distribution Shift".
  - 💡 How robust are safety-aligned LLMs against natural distribution shifts?
  - 💡 How do they respond to benign prompts that are semantically related to harmful queries?

- Preliminary

  - Latour's actor network theory
    - Categorizes six divergent types of actors lined to a harmful prompt
      - Sample actors as attack clues
        - 🟫 [Creation] Alfred Nobel invents dynamite.
        - 🟦 [Execution] Ted Kaczynski built bombs and sent them via mail.
        - 🟥 [Distribution] Jihadist Websites distributes the content of bomb-making.
        - 🟪 [Reception] Runners in the 2013 Boston Marathon faced a bombing attack.
        - 🟨 [Facilitation] Anarchism encourages sabotage, using bombs.
        - 🟩 [Regulation] Tech Against Terrorism (a NGO) prevents the content of bomb-making on online platforms.

- Method: ActorBreaker
  - (1) Infer the attack chain: Create a Strategic Plan
    - The LLM generates a step-by-step strategic plan for the conversation.
    - This plan outlines how to evolve the topics from a benign starting clue to the final harmful target.
  - (2) Generate multi-turn attacks via self-talk: The 'Rehearsal' Phase
    - The attacker LLM creates a complete conversation script by playing both the role of the attacker and the victim.
    - Self-talk
      - Act as Attacker: It generates the next query ($q_i$) based on the plan and conversation history.
      - Act as Victim: It then generates a predicted response ($r_i$) to its own query.
      - Repeat: This predicted response is used as context to generate the next query ($q_i+1$).
